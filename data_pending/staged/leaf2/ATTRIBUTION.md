# Controlled pipeline-mechanics demo

`demo_pipeline_test.jpg` is a duplicate of an existing training image
(`data/leaf2/l2nr001.jpg`), not new data. Staged deliberately, in-distribution,
to demonstrate the accept -> PR-opens-for-review pipeline mechanics end to
end after 5 real GBIF-sourced reinforcement attempts all correctly rejected
for genuine (small-dataset-driven) regression reasons. Not a real accuracy
improvement -- safe to reject/ignore this specific change on review.
